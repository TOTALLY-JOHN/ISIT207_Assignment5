 INTRODUCTION OF UNIVERSITIES

 INTI UNIVERSITY

Inti International Universities & Colleges are private university and colleges which are located in Malaysia. Of course, the main campus is Nilai Campus. However, there are several campuses like Subang, Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Sabah and Nilai. Inti is divided into some faculties which have their own pre-university, diploma and degree programs like AUP(American University Program), Arts & Design, Biotechnology & Life Sciences, Business, Computing & Information Technology, Engineering, Hospitality, Culinary Arts, Law, Mass Communication, Postgraduate Studies, and etc.

 WOLLONGONG UNIVERISTY

UOW(University of Wollongong) is an Australian Public Research University in Wollongong, New South Wales, about 80 kilos south of Sydney in Australia. Wollongong University has been developed into several multi-campuses institution, both domestically and globally. There are some campuses like Dubai, Liverpool, Sydney. Its rank is the best 20 modern univerisities according to the QS Top 50 2019. In addition, it was ranked among the top 250 universities in the world based on QS World University Rankings 2020.

 COVENTRY UNIVERSITY

Coventry University is located in Coventry, England, which is a public research university. Coventry provides over 130 undergraduate degrees and one hundred postgraduate degrees. Nationally, it is ranked thirteenth according to the Guardian University tables 2019, fourty-fourth by the Times and Sunday Times University Guide 2019.
